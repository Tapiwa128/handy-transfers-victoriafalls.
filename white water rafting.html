Jet Boat

The Adventure Jetboat, is one of the wildest river excursions you can experience during Victoria Falls Tours. It allows you to feel the intensity of the falls as you jaunt along the Zambezi River at an incredible 40km/hour. The thrill of the wind on your face, the splash from the water and not to mention the iconic view of the falls from below is what gets people excited for this Victoria Falls Activity.Ready to feel the adrenaline coursing through you while our Adventure Jet boat captains plot a course through the Zambezi? The Adventure Jet Boat Victoria Falls is an alternative to the white-water rafting trip. This is a face-paced ride on the rapids in the Zambezi River below the Victoria Falls.

White Water Rafting

Our white water rafting tours offer an adrenaline-pumping adventure for thrill-seekers and nature enthusiasts alike. With expert guides leading the way, you'll navigate through Class IV and V rapids, taking on heart-pounding drops and powerful currents as you make your way down the mighty Zambezi River. The stunning scenery of Victoria Falls adds to the excitement, with towering cliffs, lush rainforests, and breathtaking views of the iconic waterfall providing a stunning backdrop to your rafting adventure.

Kayaking & Canoeing

Paddle through the Zambezi River and take in the stunning views of the surrounding landscapes, including the iconic Victoria Falls. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced paddler, there are options for everyone to enjoy this exhilarating water sport. With experienced guides leading the way, you'll have the opportunity to navigate through calm waters and exciting rapids, all while taking in the beauty of the African wilderness. Keep an eye out for wildlife along the riverbanks, as you may spot elephants, hippos, and a variety of bird species. Whether you choose to embark on a half-day excursion or a multi-day adventure, kayaking and canoeing in Victoria Falls is sure to be a highlight of your trip. So grab your paddle and get ready for an unforgettable experience on the water!
