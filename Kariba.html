Kariba

Welcome to Kariba, Zimbabwe! Handy Taxis & Transfares and Tours is thrilled to offer you an unforgettable experience in this beautiful destination. Kariba is a town located on the shores of Lake Kariba, one of the largest man-made lakes in the world. This picturesque town is surrounded by stunning natural beauty, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers. One of the main attractions in Kariba is Lake Kariba itself. Visitors can enjoy a variety of water-based activities such as fishing, boating, and sunset cruises. The lake is also home to a diverse range of wildlife, including hippos, crocodiles, and a wide variety of bird species. For those interested in cultural experiences, Kariba offers opportunities to visit local villages and interact with the friendly residents. You can learn about traditional customs and crafts, as well as sample delicious local cuisine. Kariba is also home to the iconic Kariba Dam, which provides hydroelectric power to both Zimbabwe and Zambia. Visitors can take guided tours of the dam and learn about its fascinating history and engineering marvels. At Handy Taxis & Transfares and Tours, we offer a range of packages for exploring Kariba and its surrounding areas. Whether you're interested in a relaxing getaway or an action-packed adventure, we have something for everyone. Our knowledgeable guides are passionate about showcasing the best that Kariba has to offer, ensuring that your trip is both enjoyable and informative. We also provide comfortable accommodations and reliable transportation to make your stay as seamless as possible. So why wait? Contact Handy Taxis & Transfares and Tours today to start planning your dream trip to Kariba, Zimbabwe. We can't wait to help you create memories that will last a lifetime!
